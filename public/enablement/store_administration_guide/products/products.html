Products

Product management begins with creation of products and building a product catalog.

Most products are not just a single item and often one product comes in different shapes and sizes. Therefore, the first level of the catalog is an abstract level where we set up the common attributes of a product. This product has a price and cannot be sold and is referred to as an abstract product. On the basis of an abstract product actual inventory, comprising concrete products, can be built. Concrete products have an exact quantity and the quantity reflects your physical inventory. You can also combine two or more products together to sell them as one. Such combination of products sold together is called a product bundle. In this article we will tell you how to create and edit abstract, concrete and bundle products as well as manage their attributes.

Creating Products

To create a new product, go to Administration Interface > Products>Products and click Create Product button in the top right corner. You will be taken to Create a Product page where you can add all the details for your product. This page includes several tabs: General, Price & Tax, Variants, SEO, Images.

General Tab

The General tab provides the main options required to create a new abstract product in just a few clicks. The instruction below will help you do that.

  1. If you have multiple stores, first of all define, what store(s) you want your new product be visible in. Just select necessary stores under Store relation.

  2. Then set the desired SKU prefix for your product.

  3. Give your product a name and provide a description. These are the product name and description your customers will see on the webiste. If applicable for your shop, make sure you expand de_DE section to enter product name and description in German as well.

  4. If you want to define the time when you products should be (in)valid and (in)visible for customers in the shop, set this time in Valid from and Valid to fields. You might definitely want to populate these fields if your products have expiry date, of if these are seasonal products or products that are valid for a certain period of time (e.g. tickets), or if for promotional reasons, you temporally sell the products within a limited time frame, or if you want to start selling some products (e.g. a collection) from a specific date and/or time. Please note, that specified validity dates have priority over the manual (de)activation of product, therefore even if you have a product deactivated, but with "Valid from" and Valid to" values specified, the product will still be shown on the website until the "Valid to" date is reached. See the detailed use cases to accurately specify the desired validity period for your new product(s).

  5. Finally click Save button to save the product, or Next button to proceed to the next Price & Tax tab.
